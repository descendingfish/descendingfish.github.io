Welcome to Clash Royale

Clash Royale is a real-time multiplayer battle game developed by Supercell. Combining elements of collectible card games, tower defense, and multiplayer online battle arena, Clash Royale offers an exciting and strategic gaming experience.

Players collect and upgrade cards featuring Clash of Clans troops, spells, and defenses, then use them to battle opponents in fast-paced, three-minute matches. The goal? Destroy your opponent's towers while defending your own!

With over 100 unique cards to unlock and master, countless strategic combinations, and a vibrant competitive scene, Clash Royale continues to captivate millions of players worldwide since its launch in 2016.
