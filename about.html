About These Trees

Each tree in this collection was planted for its unique shape, color, and contribution to the landscape’s year-round beauty.

  • Japanese Maple (Red): Known for its deep red leaves and elegant shape, perfect for autumn beauty.
  • Shaped Pine Tree: Pruned into cloud-like forms, representing balance and patience in design.
  • Golden Japanese Maple: A bright yellow-green variety that glows in the sunlight during fall.
